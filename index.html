Capture the Moment

Blendie creates double exposures by taking pictures with both cameras on your iOS or Android device.

1. Take a Selfie

Take a dynamic self portrait that complements or contrasts the scenery around you.

2. Take a Picture

Capture the scenery and landscape around you in an intriguing and interesting way.

3. Swipe for Blends

Choose from the hand-selected blend modes for creating great double exposures.
